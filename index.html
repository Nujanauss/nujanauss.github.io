Your task is to maximise your score in a maze. As you move through the maze, rewards will reveal themselves. Most of the maze is empty. There may be more than one kind of reward. You may revisit the same reward as many times as you like to maximise your reward.
You will begin the task with £5. Every 10 points you score is equivalent to 10p. If you score negative points, your £5 will reduce. The lowest attainable reward is £0 (you will not receive negative money).
